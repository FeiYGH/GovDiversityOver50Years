    About this project and the data that it represents

    These reports represent the changes in diversity of our government over the last 50 years. By observing changes in our government at the federal, state, and local levels, we were able to compile an extensive dataset detailing different demographic information. This dataset defines diversity through a variety of parameters, from race and gender to age and previous occupation. With this information, we hope to determine the future trends of diversity in our governmental landscape.
